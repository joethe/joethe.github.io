Software Design and Development

Setup and Support

2014-2016

Summary

Following my completion of 3601: Software Design and Development in the Fall of 2014, I undertook a directed study in which I supported the Spring 2015 offering of that same course. I did this primarily by perparing project templates for the students' labs, as well as by helping my professor research the tools that the students would be using in their project and talking with the customer that the students would be working with in order to design their labs to teach concepts that would be critical during later work on their project.

Goal(s)

To assist my professor in preparing materials for the Spring 2015 offering of Software Design and Development, as well as with ensuring that students questions are answered during labs.

Languages and Tools

  • Stack: The MEAN stack (Mongo, Express, Angular, and Node.js)
  • IDE: Webstorm.
  • Project Generation Yeoman, Angular-Fullstack-Generator
  • Development and Testing: Grunt, Karma, Jasmine.
  • Continuous Integration: Travis, Heroku.

Specifics

A fair portion of my work consisted of putting together a series of labs which would do a good job of teaching students
