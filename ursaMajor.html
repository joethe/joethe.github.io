Software Design and Development Project

"URSA Major"

Summary

As part of the 3601: Software Design and Development course at UMM, students spend the majority of the semester working on designing and developing a project for a customer. The process follows the agile approach, with day-long inception decks and everything.

Details of the project that I worked on (with my classmates) are as follows:

  • Customer(s): The person(s) responsible for managing URS at UMM
  • Goal: To create a web app capable of managing the submission of URS abstracts and the organization of the URS event
  • Timeframe: A little under 1 semester (about 6 weeks)

Tools

  • Stack: The MEAN stack (Mongo, Express, Angular, and Node.js)
  • Development and Testing: Grunt, Karma, Jasmine.
  • Continuous Integration: Travis, Heroku.
